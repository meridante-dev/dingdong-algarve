The founders

We made this journey ourselves

Roam & Root is Alco and Nellie — a Dutch family who left the Netherlands, put down roots in the western Algarve and never looked back. Alco walks every step with you personally: he listens first, then searches relentlessly — on the market, off the market, and through the quiet networks where the Algarve's best homes change hands.

  • A personal search shaped around your life, not a listings feed
  • Honest guidance on land, light, licences and true value
