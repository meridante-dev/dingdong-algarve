The idea

Buying a home should feel like an adventure — not a transaction.

Most people picture estate agents as stiff, pushy and hard to approach. I do it the other way around: a genuinely high standard, an easy, human relationship, and a search that's actually a pleasure. You bring the dream. I bring the local knowledge, the honesty, and an assistant that hunts for your home around the clock.

How it works

Four steps to the right front door.

Everything before the viewing runs quietly in the background. When it's time for the human part, I'm there.

01

Tell me your dream

A few relaxed questions: where, how much, what matters most, and when. Two minutes, and your search is alive.

02

It searches every Portuguese portal, day and night, and matches new listings to you — so nothing good slips past.

03

We visit the best, together

I go with you to the homes worth your time — and tell you the truth about each: the beautiful, the practical, the flaws.

04

I guide you to the deed

Financing, taxes, total cost, lawyer and notary — the whole package, handled, until the keys are yours.

The difference

An assistant that never sleeps.

Other agents wait for listings to land in their inbox. Mine goes looking. The moment you tell me your dream, an AI builds your profile and searches every property portal in Portugal — continuously. Every two weeks, you get a short, curated list of homes that genuinely fit you, each with a personal note on why. You stay warm between viewings, and you never miss the one.
